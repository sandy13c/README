Ingredients

3/4 tsp kosher salt
1 pound(s) ground sirloin
1/2 pound ground Sirloin
1/4 cup dry bread Crumbs
1/2 Cup steak sauce
1 large egg
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
4 hamburger buns
4 ounces sliced blue cheese

Preparation
1Step 1: In a bowl, add chuck, sirloin, bread crumbs, steak sauce, eggs, salt, and pepper, and mix gently with fork.
2Step 2: Form 4 patties out of this mixture.
3Step 3: Cook the hamburgers patties on each side, then cover with aluminium foil.
4Step 4: Cut the sides of the bun, then grill in a stove-top grill until toasted.
5Step 5: Place patties and a slice of blue cheese in each bun.
6Step 6: Serve hot.
